**social-media-checklist.md**







**Social Media Do's:**



1.Use Privacy Settings: Regularly update your account settings to control who can see your personal posts and information.



2.Verify Information: Double-check facts from reliable sources before sharing news or viral posts to prevent spreading misinformation.



3.Network Professionally: Use platforms to connect with industry experts, alumni, and professors to build a positive career network.



4.Keep it Respectful: Always use a polite and professional tone when commenting or engaging in online discussions.



5.Think Before You Post: Ensure that every photo or status update reflects the professional image you want to show to future employers.







**Social Media Don'ts:**



1.Avoid Cyberbullying: Never post hurtful comments, share embarrassing photos of others, or engage in online harassment.



2.Don't Share Private Details: Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or any financial details like OTPs or UPI IDs.



3.Avoid Offensive Content: Do not post or share hate speech, illegal material, or inappropriate images that violate college policies.



4.Don't Click Suspicious Links: Avoid clicking on "free prize" links or urgent messages that look like phishing attempts.



5.Don't Over-share Location: Avoid tagging your exact live location on public posts for your personal safety.
