**Professional Email \& Etiquette Guide**





**Communication Scenario and Learnings:**



In this task, I focused on mastering professional communication, a core responsibility of a Student Digital Ambassador. I drafted two distinct emails: one requesting an assignment extension from a professor and another expressing interest in a summer internship. Through this exercise, I learned that professional emails are evaluated on their subject line quality, tone, and structure rather than their length. I ensured both drafts included a clear subject line, a formal greeting, a well-structured body, and a proper sign-off.



Additionally, I developed a "Social Media Do’s and Don’ts" checklist to help my peers maintain a responsible online presence. A common situation where poor digital communication causes real-world problems is when a student uses "text-speak" or an overly casual tone in an academic email, leading to misunderstandings or a loss of credibility. I now understand that clear and respectful communication is vital for building a professional reputation. This task taught me that practicing proper etiquette is as much about protecting my digital footprint as it is about effective messaging.
