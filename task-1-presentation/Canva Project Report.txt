Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

The Tool Used: I used Canva, a browser-based design tool, to create a one-page visual resource for my batchmates.

What the Design Shows: My infographic focuses on three key areas of digital literacy:
 
           Defining Digital Literacy: Explaining how to find and evaluate information online.
 
 Safe Internet Practices: Providing tips on password security and avoiding      suspicious links.
           Professional Online Presence: Highlighting the importance of platforms like LinkedIn for students.Reflection: I found it interesting how a simple template could make professional design accessible even without prior experience. One challenge I faced was selecting the most important information to ensure the poster remained easy to read and visually appealing. This task helped me realize that as a Student Digital Ambassador, visual communication is a powerful way to share knowledge with peers.
